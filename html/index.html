    • Who are we?

    We are a bunch of students with a great passion about board games. These days, everybody is connected through the internet, new platforms of communication are launched every day, each one with one specific purpose: connect people who read manga, connect horse lover...

    We've seen very different topics in millions of platforms around the globe, allowing global connections, but also a better organisation among local communities.

    No need to go any further, here in Horsens, different Facebook groups have been stablished for things such as selling second hand bicycles or sharing useful infromation with new international students that arrive every year.

    However, despite this great organisation in the internet, we found a whole that no platform was able to cover. What about meeting and playing some videogames with friends?
